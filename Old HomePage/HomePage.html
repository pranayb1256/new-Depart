OVERVIEW OF COURSE:

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning), established in the academic year 2020-21, began with an intake of 60 students in its undergraduate program. The department is supported by highly qualified and experienced faculty, along with modern, fully equipped laboratories.

The B.E. program covers a comprehensive range of subjects, including algorithm design, programming languages, software design, computer hardware, as well as courses in electronics, applied sciences, and linguistics.

Focusing on cutting-edge AI and Data Science technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Robotics, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning, the program prepares students to develop intelligent systems.

With the increasing global demand for AI and ML expertise, graduates are well-positioned for promising career opportunities in top industries worldwide.
