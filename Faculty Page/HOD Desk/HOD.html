Message from the HOD

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning). The Department presently offers a four years B.E programme in Computer Science with specializations in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. AI & ML is the most conspicuous technology that is instrumental in transforming the facet of industry and mankind. As a result, the demand of professionals trained in AI & ML is exponentially rising. The department aims to produce skilled professional in the domain of AI and ML with a strong background knowledge of computer science that will enable them to excel professionally. I feel privileged and proud to be leading a team of committed, talented and experienced faculty members and staff. In addition to the qualified faculty, the department also provides state of the art laboratory facilities to the student so that they can carry out real world multidisciplinary projects in an effective manner. The department strongly trusts in maximizing the interaction with industry and various research organizations. Overall, the comprehensive industrial exposure integrated with application and project based learning pedagogy shall make the students industry ready. The department strongly believes that our students will make us proud in the time ahead by emerging as an asset to the department, institute and society.
